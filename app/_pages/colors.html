Brand Colors

Brand colors are lightly spread through the framework. Only to keep small items consistent. They are primarily there to function as a key to use wherever inside or out of the framework.

Variables

There are three actionable brand color variables that are dispersed through the framework slightly.

$brand-color-primary
#5F81E4
$brand-color-secondary
#F67FF5
$brand-color-tertiary
#47E4E0
$brand-color-gray
#E4E4E4

{% highlight scss %} $brand-color-primary: #5F81E4; $brand-color-secondary: #F67FF5; $brand-color-tertiary: #47E4E0; $brad-color-gray: #E4E4E4; {% endhighlight %}

Flash messages, or alerts, inform users of successful or pending actions.

Usage

{% highlight scss %} @include alerts(); {% endhighlight %}

Options

Change how alerts are viewed.

{% highlight scss %} @include alerts(( name: 'alert', padding: 0.5rem 1rem, margin: $gutter-width 0, border-width: 1px, radius: 0, )); {% endhighlight %}
